## 📦 What Was Created

A complete, **elite SaaS-level React dashboard** for lead recovery and automated appointment booking with:

- ✅ **5 Core Components** (LeadCard, RecoveryFunnel, AIConversationMirror, CalendarSyncStatus, Dashboard)
- ✅ **2 Custom Hooks** (useAsync, useLocalStorage)
- ✅ **API Integration Layer** (Pre-configured for Node.js backend)
- ✅ **Mock Data Generator** (For instant development/demo)
- ✅ **Tailwind CSS Setup** (Dark obsidian theme with electric indigo accents)
- ✅ **Framer Motion Animations** (Smooth, premium feel)
- ✅ **Recharts Data Visualization** (Conversion funnels, analytics)
- ✅ **Responsive Design** (Mobile-first, desktop-optimized)
- ✅ **Glassmorphism Effects** (Frosted glass cards and modals)
- ✅ **Full Documentation** (6 comprehensive guides)
